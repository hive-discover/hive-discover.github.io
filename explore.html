How to get started

Because we value and protect your privacy, each new user must consent to our processing. This action is transparently logged in the HIVE blockchain.

1. Privacy Policy

First of all, you need to read and understand our privacy policy. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us!

2. Log Acceptance

After that, your acceptance must be documented. All you have to do is confirm a CustomJSON transaction via HiveSigner. Enter your Username below:

Username

3. Login

Now you have to wait a few seconds for the HIVE blockchain to record your transaction and for us to see it. After that, just grant us an access token via HiveSigner (Reading Only)!
